About Us

GOHackathon

Go Hackathon comprises of eminent Industry professionals, educators and students meant to nurture talent among budding tech professional.
Industry today is looking for talented students who are equipped and experience with advanced skills, software, attitude and updated industry best practices.
We create an eco-system of industry academia partnership and help in continuous and sustainable development of innovative ideas, which are impactful for the growing economy and society.
Go hackathon thrives to provide best nurturing, mentorship, skills and professional opportunities to its members and participants. We conduct series of events, seminars, hackathons, internships and Projects.
