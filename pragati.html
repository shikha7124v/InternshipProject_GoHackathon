Abstract

So we have decided to create such a product whose creation cost is minimal.

Our idea is to create such an organic and less costly product that could be fruitful for the farmers.

As we know that the flowers which are used in May be temples, marriages, or maybe waste flowers which are of no use. collect all those flowers and dump them in compost for a maximum of 7 days and then consume the liquid out of it. Which could be used as manure for farmers, gardeners, etc. So this could be an organic way to help farmers grow crops and this is very less costly and very helpful because it would be without any waste…..Apart from that there is just one more concern where some extra step should be taken to take care of this project, which is – while dumping the flowers or leaves into the compost we have to find such a place where there would be no one..may be on the terrace because it would smell bad. But we do have an alternative for the same, there are organic odors present in the market may be Jasmine or lavender flavor bottles we can use them.. and thus it is helpful and effective for the farmers without even spending much.

This is natural (KHAAD). Many researchers have stated that all these chemicals are very harmful when we consume them or even it deteriorates the quality of the soil. But this is a natural product. This liquid is also used in research work.

While gardening gardeners prefer natural/ organic things to be poured in the flowers or to grow the crop..

So this is the best thing which could be used and created without any wastage and without also cost-effectively.
